EcoCollective will be home to an interactive community.

Users will be able to add people to their digital online community. As you add people their houses will be added next to yours, forming a little online community.

The "Learn" section, will be where you can discover sustainable practices to make a positive impact on the environment. Explore topics such as reducing waste, conserving energy, and ethical consumerism.

The "Take action" section, will be where users can find thing around their local community to take part in.

The "Share" section, will allow for users to share post to their online community. Allowing others to see what they've been doing within their local community.
