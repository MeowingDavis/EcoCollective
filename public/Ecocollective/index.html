Info

Eco-Collective is a digital media platform designed to promote awareness of environmental issues and facilitate user engagement with sustainable practices. The website offers educational resources on various environmental issues and provides actionable steps that users can take to reduce their environmental impact. Additionally, users can connect with like-minded individuals to exchange knowledge and information, contributing to a collective transactive memory. Eco-Collective aims to create a community of individuals and organizations working towards a common goal of creating a sustainable future.
